Short circuit capacity of the connected AC grid is important for the selection of converter. Since the mismatch of power injection is compensated by the DC voltage controlling station, there could be sudden change in the power set-points.
Hence, if the short circuit capacity of the connected AC grid is low, then there occurs some disturbance in the AC grid every time the power set-point is changed. In case the short circuit capacity of the DC voltage controlling station is high enough, then the change in set-points in the power to compensate for the mismatch in DC grid will not have any effect on the AC grid
